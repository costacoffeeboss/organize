How it works

Three pieces. One place.

No more juggling five apps. Atomic brings the essentials of an organised life together — then quietly helps you stay on top of them.

01

Capture the atoms

Log habits, jot a journal entry, drop tasks onto your list and calendar. Everything you need to run your day lives in a single, quiet interface.

02

Build the momentum

Streaks, gentle reminders and progress rings keep the small things consistent. Tiny actions, repeated, are where real change actually comes from.
